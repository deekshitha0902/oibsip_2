Swami Vivekananda

Swami Vivekananda was an Indian Hindu monk,philosopher,author and one of the most celebrated spiritual leaders of India.

About the Legend

☛ He was the first Hindu monk to introduce Vedanta and Hinduism to the West in 1893 at the first Parliament of the World’s religions at Chicago.

☛ Globally he has been recognized as Light of India. Like Bhudha called as light of Asia.

☛ Most of his teachings are focused on the subject ABHI (fearlessness). The one word ABHI he taken from Upanishads and explained in detail.

☛ His concentration was unbelievable. During his childhood, when he and his peers were doing meditation, a snake came in the room and everybody started shouting and came out. They noticed that Vivekananda was not there. When they went inside they found that Vivekananda was not moved an inch, he was doing his meditation unaware of what was going around him.

☛ Spirituality in action, without the limitations of ritualised tradition, is what Swami Vivekananda accomplished.

☛ The realizations gained from Ramakrishna powered by his personality and pragmatism broke the stereotype prelavent even to this day.

☛ Many of the scientists such as Tesla , businessman like Tata and freedom fighter like M.Gandhi had influence of Swami Vivekananda in their own life.

☛ Today his establishment Ramakrishna mission that was started by him inspired by his Guru Ramakrishna Paramhansa , is present across the world and people are coming to connect themselves with it to get better lifestyle through Vedanta philosophy .

☛ He proved that along with the faith in God we should work hard to achieve our goal. Like he knew Sri Ramakrishna is God Himself even then he worked hard throughout His life for His Guru.

☛ He showed us the path to protect ourselves from the so called babas and incarnations by teaching us judge everything before accepting.

☛ He taught us to work unselfishly .

☛ He showed us the path to salvation.

☛ He brought out the four yogas distinctly from the Bhagavadgita: Karma Yoga, Raja Yoga, Bhakti Yoga, and Jnana Yoga.

☛ Every one can draw a different philosophy from Swami Vivekananda. He is so broad, and yet razor sharp in his every idea across the spectrum. The central teaching of Swami Vivekananda is the divinity of man (woman included of course). To put it in a more general way, the divinity of a human being.

☛ Swami Vivekananda says that the human being is the greatest being and the human body is the greatest body. He says, the human body is the Taj Mahal of all temples.

☛ Apart from the usual religions of the world that we all know, Swami Vivekananda has introduced into our midst this Religion of Fearlessness. This is the essence of all religions!
